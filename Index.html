About Us

Your Partner in Data-Driven Growth

Ubuntu Data Projects is a leading data analytics firm based in Johannesburg, South Africa. Our mission is to empower South African businesses with actionable insights derived from data, fostering sustainable growth and competitive advantage. Founded in 2021 by Nkanyiso Dube and Thabiso Ndebele, we bring together a team of experienced data scientists, analysts, and engineers dedicated to solving complex business challenges through data- driven solutions. We believe in the transformative power of data and are committed to helping our clients unlock their full potential.
